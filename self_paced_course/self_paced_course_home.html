Introduction

This is a self paced course in which the learner will be introduced to AI, and different technologies that work with AI. The course is broken down into 3 modules.

By the end of this course, learners will be able to:


• Identify AI Applications

• Identify present and future AI Applications

• Understand the process of building AI Applications

Introduction to AI

This module serves as an Introduction to what AI is and what are the emerging technologies revolving AI

Understanding AI

Understand what is inside Artifitial Intelligence and learn about AI tools - Teachable Machine/ Intel Dev cloud/ Intel Open VINO. Examine and discuss the impacts of AI in present and in future.

Plan AI

Learn about the importance of AI Ethics and responsible AI development. Devise SMART plans using SMART goal methodology.
